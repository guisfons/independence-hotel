HISTORIC BUILDING

With its streamlined, New Khmer architecture, the “Grande Dame” of Cambodian hospitality reflects the optimism of our country’s Golden Age. And she’s younger than ever! Feel the history in a contemporary, state-of-the-art environment.

VILLAS

In a more secluded part of Independence Hotel, right by the seashore and amidst tropical gardens, we offer you an array of semi-attached accommodations or private villas, some of which come with their own plunge pool to provide an exclusive beach resort experience. THE PODS VILLAGE

The Pod Village is a cluster of individual accommodation pods, developed by Independence Hotel within the grounds of the property, offering a unique eco-friendly experience surrounded by the natural jungle rainforest setting while still having complete access to all the resort's many services, like restaurants, the spa, pools, and the private beach.

Eco-Pod

The Eco-Pods are located in the Pods Village, separated and surrounded by the untouched tropical rainforest, away from the main buildings, while still giving you access to all the hotel’s facilities. Our Eco-Pods have a double bed, a bathroom, and a patio that you can use to hang out amid the green landscape.
